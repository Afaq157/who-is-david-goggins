# Who Is David Goggins?

**[Who is David Goggins](https://lifecraftr.com/david-goggins-biography/)?** David Goggins is an American ultramarathon runner, ultra-distance cyclist, motivational speaker, author, and retired Navy SEAL. He is widely recognized for his incredible mental toughness, discipline, and the ability to push human limits, inspiring millions of people worldwide.

## Biography
David Goggins was born in 1975 in the United States. He had a challenging childhood, facing poverty, abuse, and learning difficulties. Despite these obstacles, he transformed himself through sheer determination, eventually becoming a Navy SEAL and one of the most elite endurance athletes in the world.

## Achievements
- Completed more than 60 ultra-endurance races  
- Set a world record for the most pull-ups in 24 hours  
- Retired Navy SEAL  
- Bestselling author of *Can’t Hurt Me*  
- Motivational speaker and influencer in personal development

## Philosophy & Mindset
David Goggins’ approach is based on **mental toughness, discipline, and accountability**. His key principles include:  
- Embrace discomfort and push past your limits  
- Take responsibility for your life and actions  
- Build a “calloused mind” through daily challenges  
- Use adversity as fuel to grow stronger
